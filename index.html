The Intersection of Engineering and Education

My journey began as a Mechatronics Engineer, giving me a deep, practical understanding of mechanics, electronics, and control systems. This engineering mindset is the foundation of my teaching philosophy: Physics is not just theory; it's the operating manual for the universe.

For the past several years, I've had the privilege of teaching high school Physics at various educational centers, focusing on innovative, center-based methodologies to ensure every student, 'El-Ghalaba' included, grasps even the most complex concepts. My goal is always to make learning engaging, memorable, and ultimately, simple.
